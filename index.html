How to Use This Dashboard

SAY means read this out loud.

DO means leader directions only. Do not read out loud.

COPY TO CHAT means use the copy button and paste into Zoom chat.

NOTE means helpful reminder or tech tip.

✅
Do

Before the Meeting Starts

Have these pages open before the meeting begins:

  1. This leader dashboard.
  2. Chapter 5, How It Works, in case someone else cannot read or you need to paste the link in chat.
  3. AA Daily Reflections, so you can copy and paste the day’s reflection link.

You do not need to claim host or ask for readers before the meeting starts. Those happen during the meeting.

💬
Say

1. Welcome

Good morning, welcome to the 7:30 am SunShiners meeting of AA. I am ______ and I’m an alcoholic, or however you choose to introduce yourself.

Let’s begin with a moment of silence for the still suffering alcoholic, followed by the Serenity Prayer.

Opening Serenity Prayer, I Form
God, grant me the serenity to accept the things I cannot change,
the courage to change the things I can,
and the wisdom to know the difference.

Say

2. Preamble

Alcoholics Anonymous is a fellowship of people who share their experience, strength, and hope with each other that they may solve their common problem and help others to recover from alcoholism.

The only requirement for membership is a desire to stop drinking.
